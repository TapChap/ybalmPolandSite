About This Site

This website is dedicated to documenting the journey of ישיבת בני עקיבא לפיד מודיעין's trip to Poland. Here, you will find a comprehensive gallery of photos, daily summaries of the trip, and a special letter to Poland.

The trip is a meaningful educational experience for the students, allowing them to explore their heritage, learn about the history, and connect with the past in a profound way. This site serves as a platform to share these experiences with friends, family, and the wider community.
